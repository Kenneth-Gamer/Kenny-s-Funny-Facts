    Why do Mario and Luigi wear hats?
    Arcade machines back in the 1980s were not that good at graphics.
    This would save the developers from animating hair if the characters didn't wear hats.

    Why is Python (the language) called Python?
    The creator of Python, Guido van Rossum, was reading the scripts from Monty Python's Flying Circus.
    He thought that his coding language should be simple yet mysterious.
